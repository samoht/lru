D
drop [Lru_map.F.S]
drop m is m without the binding lru m.
drop [Lru_map.M.S]
drop m drops the binding that lru m returns.

E
empty [Lru_map.F.S]

F
filter [Lru_map.F.S]
filter f m discards the bindings when f k v is false.
find [Lru_map.F.S]
find k m is the pair (v, m'), where v is the value associated to k, and m' is a map with the same bindings in m, or None, if k is not bound in m.
find [Lru_map.M.S]
find k m is the value associated to k in m, or None, if there is no such value.
find_remove [Lru_map.F.S]
find_remove k m is a pair (v, m'), where v is the binding for k, and m' does not contain a binding for k, or None, if k is not bound in m.
fmap [Lru_map.F.S]
fmap f m combines filter and map; if f k v evaluates to None, the binding is discarded.
fold [Lru_map.F.S]
fold f m s is the catamorphism.
fold [Lru_map.M.S]
fold f m s is the catamorphism.

I
is_empty [Lru_map.F.S]
is_empty m is true iff m is empty.
iter [Lru_map.F.S]
iter f m visits all bindings in m with f.
iter [Lru_map.M.S]
iter f m visits all bindings in m with f.

L
lru [Lru_map.F.S]
lru m is the least recently used binding, or None, is the map is empty.
lru [Lru_map.M.S]
lru m is the least recently used binding, or None, is the map is empty.

map [Lru_map.F.S]

mem [Lru_map.F.S]
mem k m tests whether k is bound in m.
mem [Lru_map.M.S]
mem k m tests whether k is bound in m.

pp [Lru_map.F.S]

pp [Lru_map.M.S]
pp pp_kv ppf m pretty-prints m to ppf, using pp_kv to print bindings.

R
remove [Lru_map.F.S]
remove k m is m with the binding for k removed.
remove [Lru_map.M.S]
remove k m removes the current binding for k, if there is one.

S
size [Lru_map.F.S]
size m is the size of m.
size [Lru_map.M.S]
size m is the size of m.

T
trim [Lru_map.F.S]
trim n m is a map with no more than n bindings, created from m by discarding them in the LRU order.
trim [Lru_map.M.S]
trim n m ensures that m contains no more than n mappings.
